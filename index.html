Use familiar Vim keys to navigate and modify the list.

j & k to move down or up

h & l to outdent or indent

gg & G to move to the top or bottom

dd to delete an item

x to check or uncheck it

i & Return to begin editing at the beginning

A to begin editing at the end

O & o to insert a new item above or below

Escape to stop editing and return to command mode
